Encrypticon Ltd. is a quantitative research company dedicated to the discovery
and application of automated investment strategies in a variety of instruments.
We experiment with quantitative and machine learning models used in academia
to predict performances in financial markets. We are an experimental technology company.

Our research team is comprised of graduates in mathematics and technology
from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Stanford University and Dartmouth College.
We have previously had engineers who graduated from Oxford University and Tsinghua University.
We are a multiethnic, multi-cultural team.
We like being anonymous.
